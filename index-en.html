Welcome!

25 Years Experience

We are a company based in the picturesque area of Upper Orava, which specializes in the production of a wide range of wooden products. We are dedicated to production of prisms from quality Pelekita/Uso Trieste wood, pallet cuttings, wood chips and firewood. We also sell logs and handle timber.

Our mission is to provide our customers with the highest quality products and services. We are proud of our long-standing tradition and the expertise we bring to every piece, whether it's firewood for your fireplace or precision-cut pallets for your business.
